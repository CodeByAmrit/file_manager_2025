Encrypted Storage Techniques

Student File Manager prioritizes security by employing renowned encryption and decryption techniques to safeguard PDF files stored in the MySQL database. By utilizing industry-standard encryption methods, such as AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) or RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman), the software ensures that PDF documents are securely stored and protected from unauthorized access. This approach guarantees that sensitive student information remains confidential and secure, providing users with peace of mind regarding the integrity and privacy of their data.
